GreenCore Sales Performance Dashboard

Analysed 150 sales transactions for Green Core Distribution LTD, covering 5 sales representatives, 5 product categories, and 6 regions across Nigeria. Built a fully interactive Excel dashboard and authored an executive business brief for management.

Key Findings

  • — Revenue of ₦20,967,000 against COGS of ₦17,890,000 yielded a tight profit of ₦3,077,000 — flagging a heavy cost structure.
  • — Monthly units sold declined steadily from 134 (August) to 81 (November), indicating seasonality or weakening demand.
  • — Electronics dominated category profit at ₦1,121,000; Fashion (₦561,000) and Home Appliances (₦462,000) lagged significantly.
  • — John Aluko led revenue generation at ₦5,783,000 while Peace Danjuma trailed at ₦4,829,000 — revealing a performance gap across the sales team.
  • — Low-margin products (Sugar ₦92K, Jeans ₦101K, Microwave ₦128K) were identified as candidates for pricing review or discontinuation.

Strategic Recommendations

  • — Shift focus from revenue targets to margin-led selling — track contribution per product line.
  • — Implement a sales rep performance framework with monthly rankings, coaching, and profit-linked incentives.
  • — Replicate the top-performing region's pricing, distribution channels, and customer mix into weaker regions.
